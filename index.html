This Awesome Phone

The Samsung Galaxy Note 20 Ultra is a high-end smartphone announced on August 5, 2020. It is equipped with an Exynos 990 SoC supported by 12 GB of RAM, a 6.9-inch Super AMOLED screen and a triple 108 + 12 + 12 megapixel photo sensor. It has a 4500 mAh battery and it is equipped with Android 10 with the One UI interface.
